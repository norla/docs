Block - element - modifier


Block: Standalone entity that is  meaningful on its own.
       (header, menu, checkbox, input)
       Naming: latin letters, digits and dashes
       CSS: .some-block

Element: Has no standalone meaning, part of a block
         (menu item, list item, checkbox caption)
         Naming: latin letters, digits, dashes and underscores
         CSS: .some-block__some-elem

Modifier: Flag on on block or element. Used to change look/behaviour
          (disabled, big, highlighted)
          CSS: .some-block-or-element--modifier
